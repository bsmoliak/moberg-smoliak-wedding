Our journey to this moment has twisted and turned,
paths criss-crossing the halls of Eden Prairie High School
and college in Nebraska and Vermont,
parting for years, then back together by chance and choice,
from the eastern shores of San Francisco Bay and Puget Sound
back to the Mighty Mississippi and Land of 10,000 Lakes,
circling the Twin Cities and common causes of
family, friends, bikes, gardens, and life's unsolvable mysteries.
